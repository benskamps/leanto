a table, some sticks, and gravity.
L-drag a stick — pick up & move · R-drag — rotate (spin & tilt)
scroll while holding — lift · release — it freezes where you leave it
Space new stick · B BUILD ⇄ RUN — drop it into live physics
drag empty — orbit the camera · 1–4 angles · F frame your build
G GLUE — click two sticks to bond them into a rigid assembly
S SNIP — hover a stick, click to cut it in two
D STAMP — copy the last stick at the cursor · hold + sweep to lay a course
while holding: QE spin · RF tilt · ZX roll
Shift+Space half-stick · M mute · Backspace sweep the table
R-drag empty — pan · scroll — zoom (toward the cursor)
Ctrl+Z undo · Ctrl+S save table · Ctrl+O open a saved one
P photo mode (turntable) · Shift+P save a picture · ☀︎ dial, bottom-right
The test: prop a lean-to — each stick holds where you leave it — then press B and watch it stand, or fall. Satisfying? That's the whole question.
